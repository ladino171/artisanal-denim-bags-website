**Artisanal Denim Bags Website**

📌 **Project Overview**



Institutional website created for a sustainable artisanal brand that produces unique handmade denim bags using recycled jeans.



🎯 **Objective**



To build a clean and structured digital presence for a small handmade business with limited production and exclusive pieces.



🧠 **Concept**



The website is organized into three main sections:



Available Pieces



Custom Orders



Transform Your Jeans



The goal is to communicate exclusivity, sustainability, and craftsmanship.

CSS3
